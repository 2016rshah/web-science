(inner) stripToken(token) → {string}

Remove leading and trailing punctuation from a potential url. Note: most punctuation is technically legal in urls. However, we parse potential URLs out of free-form text, where they are often followed by commas or periods, or enclosed in quotes or parantheses. Most URLs don't end in such punctuation, and we will fail to match these URLs without stripping the extraneous characters.
